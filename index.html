Visualizing Mathematical Combinations and Permutations

An interactive web app that demonstrates combinations and permutations and the difference between them.

Add or Subtract Set Items

Combinations and Permutations are made of unique items in a set. Click 'Remove Item' or 'Add Item' to make the set bigger or smaller. These items will be picked and arranged in the Permutations and Combinations below.

Permutations

A mathematical permutation is the number of different ways the items from a set (above) are arranged. In permutations, we count all the uniquely arranged items toward the final permutation number. In this case there are tk permutations for tk items.

Combinations

A mathematical combination is the number of ways the items from a set (above) are arranged excluding duplicates . In combinations, we don't count repetitions in arrangements. In this case there are tk combinations for tk items.

  • Cras justo odio
  • Dapibus ac facilisis in
  • Morbi leo risus
  • Porta ac consectetur ac
  • Vestibulum at eros
